7. Ranking, Preferred Program, Star Ratings, and Guest Reviews

Our goal is to present search results tailored to your preferences by offering a personalized default ranking of Trip Providers on our Platform. You have the flexibility to navigate through this default ranking, apply filters, and sort based on alternative criteria, granting you control over the display of search results. We utilize multiple algorithms to generate default ranking results, a process that continuously evolves. Iterwesttravels.com has identified key parameters closely associated with finding a suitable Trip Provider, prioritizing these parameters in our algorithms (main parameters). These include your personal search history, the rate of click-through from the search page to the hotel page, conversion rates (related to bookings versus visits), and the gross and net bookings of a Trip Provider. Conversion rates may be influenced by various factors, such as review scores, availability, pricing, and quality of content. Additionally, the commission percentage paid by the Trip Provider and participation in specific programs, like the Genius program and deals, can impact ranking. The Trip Provider's record of on-time payment also plays a role. Moreover, Trip Providers can enhance their ranking by participating in programs such as the Preferred Partner Program and the visibility booster, which involve paying a higher commission to us. Star ratings for accommodations on Iterwesttravels.com are determined either by the properties themselves or by independent third-party providers. Deals are displayed based on the star ratings assigned by providers to Iterwesttravels.com. Depending on local regulations, star classifications may be assigned by independent third parties or based on the accommodation provider's self-assessment. Iterwesttravels.com does not impose formal obligations on star ratings but aims to reflect how accommodations compare to legal requirements or industry standards in terms of price, facilities, and services. To assist customers in finding accommodations that match their preferences, Iterwesttravels.com may assign a quality rating, displayed as yellow tiles, to certain properties. This rating is determined by considering over 400 features across five major categories: facilities, property configuration, photo quality, guest review scores, and historical booking data. Machine learning analysis based on these inputs results in a quality rating automatically calculated and awarded to each accommodation. Only customers who have stayed at an accommodation will be invited by Iterwesttravels.com to provide feedback on their experience.
